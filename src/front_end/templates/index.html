WHAT IS PLANCTON ?

Plancton is a modern web application designed to help students efficiently organize their homework, tests, and grades. With an intuitive interface and vibrant colors, Plancton allows users to easily manage their schedules, track assignments, and stay on top of their academic goals. It's a lightweight, serverless solution focused on user privacy, storing data directly on the browser without the need for server-side authentication.
